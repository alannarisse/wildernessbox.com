Wilderness Box

Rejuvenate yourself with the Wilderness Box™! All the scientifically proven benefits of the Forest Cleanse -- the increased mindfulness, calm, and energy -- in your own home or office! The Wilderness Box™ brings you the peaceful sights and sounds of a night in the wilderness, but in complete comfort and safety! With the finest in Natural Sound Experience, the awe-inspiring artistry of stars in their constellations overhead, and a genuine forest products tree stump for seated meditation, brought to you in all-natural materials and the finest craftsmanship.

The Wilderness Box will be shown as part of the Portland Winter Light Festival. The Wilderness Box team is part of CETI Institute's Consltellations project
